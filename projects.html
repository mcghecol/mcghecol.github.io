My Work

  • CKP Production Management System
    While I was employed at Central Kentucky Processing, I created a program that would transfer their paper scheduling system into a singular C# application. The app was designed to load in traveler data that was sent from the customer, and allow CKP's production manager to electronically schedule certain jobs for specific manufacturing lines. This enabled faster and more consise scheduling along with less human errors.

  • C#
    Over the past four years, the majority of my assignments have been created through the use of MS Visual Studio and C#. Some of my projects included a responsive tic-tac-toe program that can keep score and recognize all possible outcomes. A Point of Sale system that takes the customer's order, adds the items together for an order total, and prints out a receipt. I also created an MVC application that could intake JSON from an api and output that content into a readable GUI for the user.

  • Business Communications Formal Report
    In this project, my four team members and I worked as the R&D team for our mock company called Voyage Cruise Lines. I was designated the executive editor which took each team members part and tied them together to create a concise formal report. The report included: Executive Summary, Company Scope, In Depth SWOT Analysis, Positive/Negative Industry Trends, Porter's 5 Forces Analysis, and multiple real life examples of similar cruise lines and how they handled disaster situations. At the end of the semester, we presented our formal report in front of the company's owner (our professor) and possible investors (our classmates).
