I remember my dad telling me the story of how Michael Dell started his billion dollar company from scratch by selling homeade computers out of his dorm room. This is the kind of energy that brings people together in order to drive technology forward. I really want to work with Dell because of their focus on advancing sustainability. Google has been leading the big data field for many years now. The accuracy of their algorithms and predictive technologies blow me away. As an MIS major that is interested in big data, Google is a company that I could never stop learning from. Everyone knows that knowledge is power, so having data makes someone powerful. I believe you can change the world with the massive amount of data that google possesses.
