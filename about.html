About Me

Hello, name is Collin Mcghee and I'm an MIS major at the University of Oklahoma. I was born in Jacksonville Florida but I have lived most of my life in Houston Texas. I attened high school at the Woodlands and wrestled on varsity there for four years. Once I graduated, I knew I wanted to go to a school that wasn't the same place I lived my whole life, but also find one that wasn't too far from home. When I visited OU, I knew I had found my second home. Some of my favorite things to do there are play intermurals with my brothers of Kappa Sigma, go to Crossover and Victory Family Church with my fellow OU students, and keep myself in shape by lifting at Crunch Fitness.

Why MIS

When I first arrived at OU, I had no idea where I wanted to go with my life. The only thing that I knew was that I was very personable, and wanted to work with all different kinds of people. So I started off as an International bussiness major, but as I got further and further along, I knew it wasn't for me. It was my first basic business MIS class that caught my attention. My parents would always ask me, "what classes did you enjoy taking?" and I would never have an answer. But as I went through the semester, being able to translate that once foreign language of SQL into something that I could understand was so fun. I just wanted to learn more and more about these languages that made no sense to the majority of people. As I have moved along I took more and more coding classes and I realized that this is where I wanted to grow and expand.
